About Berhanu Kefyalew

Licensed Building Electrician

Hi, I'm Berhanu Kefyalew, a licensed electrician based in Hawassa, Ethiopia with over four years of professional experience serving homeowners, contractors, and businesses.

I specialize in electrical fitting and wiring diagram installations for both residential and commercial buildings. From simple wiring jobs to complex electrical layouts, I bring safety, reliability, and professionalism to every project.

My goal is to deliver quality work that meets local electrical standards while exceeding client expectations through precision workmanship and attention to detail.
